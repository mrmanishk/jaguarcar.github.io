Information about Jaguar

A Part of Jaguar Land Rover Limited, the British origin multinational car manufacturer is a wholly owned subsidiary of Tata Motors since 2008 and is headquartered in Whitley, Coventry, United Kingdom. Founded in 1922 as the Swallow Sidecar Company, Jaguar initially made motorcycle sidecars before manufacturing passenger car bodies. Formerly known as S.S. Cars, the company produced its first car in 1935 and changed its ame to Jaguar in 1945. Tata Motors officially bought Jagaur Land Rover from Ford Motors in 2008. In India, the company has its assembly plant located in Pune, Maharashtra where some of the Jaguar cars are assembled. History Of Jaguar

On 1 January 2013, the group, which had been operating as two separate companies underwent a fundamental restructuring. The parent company was renamed to Jaguar Land Rover Automotive PLC, while the Jaguar Cars Limited was renamed to Jaguar Land Rover Limited and the assets of Land Rover were transferred to it. This ensured that Jaguar Land Rover Limited was responsible in the UK for the design, production and marketing of both Jaguar and Land Rover products.
